How to import Minecraft Modpacks?


It depends from each modpack. It could be created for Minecraft Launcher - Official, Crystal Launcher, Prism Launcher and many more!

Modpacks created for Minecraft Launcher:

Method 1:

Download your Modpack and unzip it

Open Minecraft Launcher and enter Installations



Create new Installation



Enter you data, then click browse in Game Directory tab



Create new folder (ex.: Profiles), create folder for your Modpack, drag your modpack files there and Click Upload



Confirm your data by clicking Create



Click Play and wait for the game to open



Modpacks created for Crystal Launcher:

Download your Modpack. It should have *.clpkg extension

Double-click it to open Crystal Launcher



Enter your data and Confirm



After a second your Modpack got imported! Select your Modpack



Click Play and wait



Modpacks created for Prism Launcher:

Download your Modpack. It should have *.zip or *.mrpack extension



Open Prism Launcher

Click Add Instance



Select Import, enter your data then click Browse



Select your Modpack File and Open it



Confirm everything by clicking OK



Launch your game!
